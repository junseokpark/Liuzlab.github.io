title: "Texas Children's Hospital Inaugurates a New Data Science Center"
date: 2024-03-13
summary: "Texas Children's Hospital inaugurates new Data Science Center at Duncan NRI with Dr. Liu as Chief of Computational Sciences, serving as a centralized research hub."
---
---

Texas Children’s Hospital recently inaugurated a new Data Science Center on the first floor of the Jan and Dan Duncan Neurological Research Institute (Duncan NRI) that will serve as a centralized research hub for all data scientists throughout the hospital. Dr. Zhandong Liu, Chief of Computational Sciences at Texas Children’s Hospital and Duncan NRI investigator, who is also a Ting Tsung and Wei Fong Chao Endowed Chair, and associate professor at Baylor College of Medicine, will lead the Center.
