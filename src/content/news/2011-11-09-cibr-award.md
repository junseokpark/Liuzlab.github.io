We were just awarded a Baylor College of Medicine Computational and Integrative Biomedical Research Seed Grant for a proposal entitled, "Integrating Genetic Networks to Discover Biomarkers for Glioblastoma".  The grant is for a project in collaboration with Dr. Genevera Allen.
